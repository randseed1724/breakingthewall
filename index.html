Breaking the Wall

...or buidling it

Call a friend and choose your character. Depending on your selection you will have to build or distroy the wall. Accomplish your task and win!

How to play?

1st Player

  • Key S: Build
  • Key W: Jump
  • Key A: Move Left
  • Key D: Move Right

2nd Player

  • Key Up: Destroy
  • Key Down: Hide
  • Key Left: Move Left
  • Key Right: Move Right
